Hello! I’m Jen Arevalo, a visual designer and illustrator from Jacksonville, Florida. I have an unabashed love for crafting usuable interfaces and researching user insights to improve interactions with diverse audiences. I enjoy collaborating on thought-provoking pieces and presenting high-quality information through design to help shape the overall user experience.

When I’m not working on the latest and greatest in design, I currently serve as incoming Vice President for AIGA Jacksonville, a local chapter of the nation’s largest community of design advocates. I have a passion for mentoring and advancing design in Jacksonville.
